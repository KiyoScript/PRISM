Health Thresholds

Individual rolls at or below this length show yellow warning.

Material group shows ⚠ low warning when total usable length (open remaining + sealed full length) falls below this value.

Green — Healthy (above near-empty threshold)
Yellow — Near Empty (at/below near-empty threshold)
Red — Consumed (0 ft remaining)
⚠ Low Stock — material group total usable length below threshold

Auto Behavior

Auto-Consume on Zero

Mark roll as CONSUMED when remaining length hits 0.
